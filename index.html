About the Kingdom

Located in the beautiful valley of the Phasis River with access to the Black Sea, and a perfect mild climate, the Kingdom of Colchis is unique. Weither you like sunbathing, hiking or treasure huniting, we got your back!

Our Kingdom has been blessed by the Gods and by our beloved Royal Family with wealth and knowledge. We have developped the most advanced agricultural techniques as well as skillful metal working abilities. And indeed, the Kingdom of Colchis flows with gold and precious metals.

Come to our Kingdom and relax at the beach, follow the legend of The Argonauts and, last but not least, try to win the Golden Ticket by playing our world famous Lottery!
