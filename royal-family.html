King Aetees

Beholed the glory of our Kind Aetees, son of Helios, God of the Sun, and of the nymph Perseis. First King of Corinth, he decided to leave Greece and built the Kingdom of Colchis. The Golden Fleece was given to him by Phrixos in exchange of his daughter Chalciope. King Aetees have another daughter, the beautiful Medea. He also had a lot of spouses, but by respect to Queen Idiya, we shall not name them. King Aetees has promised Jason the Golden Fleece if he could perform certain tasks, which remain to be seen.

Princess Madea

Princess Medea is the daughter of our King and our Queen. She is, hence, a direct descendant of the Sun God Helios. She also has a deep connection with Hecate, the Goddess of Magic, which explaines why she possesses magical capabilities. Some say she will be the main actor of a terrible greek tragedy. Queen Idiya

Not much is known about our beloved Queen Idiya. She is the daughter of Titan's children, Oceanus and Tethys (they are brother and sister but fear not our Queen is perfectly healthy). She has 3000 sisters known as the Oceanids and 3000 brothers known as the Patamoi. The whole family is pretty close to the water element. Queen Idiya has the reputation of being very wise and some say she could be the goddess of knowledge.
